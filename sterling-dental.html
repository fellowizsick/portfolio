Our Practice

Founded in 2010 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, Sterling Dental was built on a simple belief: exceptional dental care should feel personal, not clinical. We combine advanced technology with genuine compassion to create a space where every patient feels like family.

From state-of-the-art digital imaging to same-day crown technology, we invest in the tools and training that make your visit faster, safer, and more comfortable. Our team of specialists collaborates on every case to ensure you receive the highest standard of care.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

We recommend a checkup and professional cleaning every six months. Regular visits allow us to catch issues early, prevent decay, and maintain your oral health. Patients with gum disease or other conditions may benefit from more frequent visits — we'll customize a schedule based on your needs.
We accept most major dental insurance plans including Delta Dental, MetLife, Cigna, Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, and United Concordia. Our front office team will verify your benefits before your first visit and help you maximize your coverage. Give us a call and we'll check your plan!
Your first appointment includes a comprehensive dental exam, digital X-rays (if needed), a professional cleaning, and a personalized treatment plan. We'll review your medical history, discuss your goals, and address any concerns. The entire visit typically takes 60–90 minutes. We also offer complimentary coffee, tea, and a warm blanket to help you relax!

The implant process typically takes 3–6 months from start to finish.

FAQ

Common Questions

How fast can you build a landing page?
Typically 1-2 hours from start to live. Most single-page projects are delivered the same day.
Do you use page builders or templates?
No. Every site is hand-coded from scratch in HTML/CSS/JS. No WordPress, no Wix, no page builders — just clean, fast, custom code.
What if I need changes after delivery?
All packages include revision rounds. Additional changes can be made at a reasonable hourly rate. I'm responsive and flexible.
Do you host the website?
I deploy on Netlify or your preferred hosting (Vercel, Cloudflare, etc.). I can also set up a custom domain for you. Hosting costs are separate and typically free or very low.
What information do you need from me?
Just your business info (what you do, who you serve, what problem you solve), any brand assets (logo, colors), and a few design references if you have them. I handle the rest.

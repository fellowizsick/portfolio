FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days within the US. Expedited shipping (1-3 days) is available at checkout. International shipping takes 7-14 business days.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all items. Simply start a return through your account and ship it back — we cover the return shipping on defective items.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout. Duties and taxes may apply.
Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and we cannot guarantee changes.

Our Story

Four Generations of Passion

Born in the heart of Naples and raised in the rolling hills of Campania, Bella Napoli brings the warmth and spirit of Southern Italy to every table. Our founders, the Bianchi family, have been perfecting their craft since 1989.

We source the finest San Marzano tomatoes, hand-rolled pasta from Molise, and extra-virgin olive oil from family groves in Puglia. Every pizza is baked in our custom wood-fired oven at 900°F — just as it’s done in Naples.

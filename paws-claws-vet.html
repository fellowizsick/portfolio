About Us

Where Compassion Meets Clinical Excellence

Founded in 2008, Paws & Claws Veterinary Clinic was built on a simple belief: every tail wag and every purr matters. Our team of dedicated veterinarians and support staff bring together decades of experience, state-of-the-art technology, and an unwavering commitment to gentle, fear-free care.

From routine wellness exams to advanced surgical procedures, we treat your pets as if they were our own — with patience, kindness, and the highest medical standards.

❤️

Fear-Free Philosophy

Every visit is designed to reduce stress and build trust, with gentle handling and a calming environment.

🔬

Advanced Diagnostics

In-house lab, digital X-ray, and ultrasound for accurate, same-day results when your pet needs them most.
